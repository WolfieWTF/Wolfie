THE POSITION OF SUBMISSION

" But he giveth more grace. Wherefore he saith, God resisteth the proud, but giveth unto the humble."

James 4:6(KJV).

Many Christians today lack the abundance of grace. Although every Christian have a measure of grace, not all have grace in abundance. It is the abundance of grace that guarantees our reigning in life. If you find your situation unbearable then it proves that you lack the abundance of grace. If you cannot always bear difficult situations that you find yourself in, it provs that you don't have abundance of grace, it shows you don't have the abundance of grace. When someone offends you, you should be able to bear it. You should be able to bear the faults of your collegues work and your annoying room mate. You should be able to bear nagging and overbearing spouse else it proves that you don't have the abundance of grace. Abundance of grace sustains in difficult and painful times.


PARYERS: Heavenly Father i surrender to the Lordship of Jesus Christ in my life. I make up my mind to always go in the way of the Spirit. Thank you Father in Jesus name. Amen.
